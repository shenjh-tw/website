Biography (自傳)

Dr. Shen received the B.S. degree in Information Engineering from I-Shou University, Kaohsiung, Taiwan, in 1999, and the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from National Sun Yat-Sen University, Kaohsiung, Taiwan, in 2001 and 2008, respectively. During August 2009 and January 2015, he was an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Communication at Asia University, Taichung, Taiwan. During February 2015 and July 2022, he was an Associate Professor in the Department of Information Communication at Asia University. Since August 2022, he has been an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Management at National United University, Miaoli, Taiwan. His research interests include mobile information systems, data mining, web-based information systems, signal processing, location-based services, and wireless sensor networks.
